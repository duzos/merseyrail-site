What is Railway 200?

Railway 200 Marks the 200th anniversary of Modern Railway from 1825 and Celebrates the impact of rail travel on society and technology

The 'Inspiration' train showcases the past history of railways through exhibition carriages with 3 themes

  • Railway firsts: showcasing innovations in railway history
  • Wonderlab on wheels: Hands on activities that explore the science and engineering behind railways
  • Your railway Future: showcasing the hidden roles in rail and encouraging people to consider a career in rail
  • Hosts community events, educational activities and heritage events
  • Storytelling is encouraged through podcasts and personal stories from the public
  • Supports multiple charity partnerships with a £200,000 fundraising goal
  • Aims to inspire and educate future rail workers
